Lasagna

Description

Easy to make homemade lasagna, very beginner friendly and quick to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 1 can of tomato pulp
  • 500 grams of minced meat
  • garlic
  • union
  • one bag of cheese

Steps

  • Cook the meat and add spices as needed
  • Cut the garlic and union and add with the meat
  • Take a big bowl and layer the meat, sauce and sheets
  • Make 2 layers and on top of the last sheets add the rest of the tomato sauce
  • Now add the cheese and put in the oven on 200 degreees
  • When the cheese is brown take the meal out of the oven and enjoy
