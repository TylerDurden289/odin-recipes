Eggs and Rice

Description

Quick and very easy to make dish if you ever need a quick bite and don't have time.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • rice
  • soy sauce
  • 1 tomatoe

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water and add the rice
  2. Set a timer for the rice according to the rice package
  3. Take a pan, add some butter en cook your eggs
  4. Add spices as required
  5. When the rice is ready, take the pot of the fire and get rid of excess water
  6. Wash your tomatoe
  7. Add the rice, eggs and tomatoe on a plate
  8. Adjust the taste of the rice with soy sauce and enjoy
