Italian Ovendish

Description

Delicious and fast ovendish with tomatosauce, rice and mozarella.

Ingredients

  • 500 gram of minced meat
  • 2 pots of tomato sauce
  • rice
  • 2 bags of mozarella cheese

Steps

  • Cook the meat in a pan and boil the rice in a pot
  • Take a big baking dish and add the cooked meat in a lager at the bottom
  • On top of this add 1 layer of mozarella chees and 1 layer of rice
  • On top of this add the tomato sauce
  • Add 1 more layer of mozarella cheese
  • Put in the oven on 200 degrees until brown
